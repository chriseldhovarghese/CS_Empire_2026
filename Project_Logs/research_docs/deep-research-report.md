# Top UK Universities (CS/AI)  

**University of Cambridge – Computer Science (BA, MEng):** Cambridge’s CompSci Tripos is highly selective (≈6 apps/place, ~17% admission【25†L257-L264】). It offers the *Computer Science BA/MEng* (no separate AI BSc). The department hosts **Machine Learning & AI** and **Mobile Systems, Robotics & Automation** research groups【23†L190-L199】. Undergraduate research opportunities include the **Accelerate Programme for Scientific Discovery** (AI/ML summer research training)【32†L442-L450】 and Google’s *DeepMind Research Ready* paid summer internship【34†L430-L438】. Entry requires A*A*A at A-level (A* in Mathematics)【16†L497-L505】 and *TMUA* (Test of Mathematicians for University Admission)【16†L583-L591】. Indian students cannot qualify on CBSE alone; they typically need high AP/IB/A-level scores (e.g. 5 APs with 5s plus CBSE) as Cambridge does **not** admit on Class XII alone【30†L972-L979】. International tuition is about **£44,214**/yr (2026 rate)【37†L1-L4】. Scholarships are mostly partial: the **Cambridge Trust (CET)** offers bursaries (rarely full)【39†L261-L270】 and colleges have limited awards【39†L273-L281】. *Requires TMUA.*  

**University of Oxford – Computer Science (BA/MCompSci):** Oxford admits ~7% of CS applicants【41†L142-L148】. It offers *Computer Science BA* (3yr) and an integrated *MCompSci* (4yr). Key AI-related labs include the **Oxford Robotics Institute (ORI)** and its **Machine Learning Research Group**【64†L103-L111】【66†L32-L41】. No separate AI degree exists, but final-year students do individual projects. Entry is A*AA (with A*A in Mathematics and Further Mathematics)【41†L126-L134】 and the *TMUA* is required【41†L137-L145】. Indian applicants need top board grades: for CBSE, typically A1,A1,A1,A1,A2 (with A1 in Maths/CS)【46†L1268-L1272】 (CISCE: ≈90%+). Oxford’s overseas tuition is up to **£62,820**/yr (2026)【53†L195-L203】. Scholarships for internationals are few (some college bursaries or external awards); largely students fund themselves. *Requires TMUA (or MAT/PAT depending on course, but TMUA is listed for CS【41†L137-L145】).*  

**Imperial College London – Computing BEng/MEng:** Imperial offers BEng/MEng *Computing* (with streams in AI & Machine Learning, Visual Computing & Robotics, etc) and a dedicated BEng *Mathematics & Computer Science for AI*【81†L319-L326】. Its labs include the **Robot Intelligence Lab** (robotics+ML), an Adaptive Robotics lab, and others. Undergrads do final-year projects and there is co-op/placement. Entry requires A*AA (A* in Math, Further Math)【81†L398-L404】; from 2024 all applicants must take the *TMUA*【72†L27-L32】. Indian applicants need very high marks (e.g. CBSE ~85–90% in PCM) plus strong SAT/ACT or AP results. Imperial’s acceptance is very low (~5%【81†L297-L304】). International tuition is **£45,500+**/yr【81†L344-L349】. Scholarships are limited (e.g. Imperial President’s Scholarship) and often highly competitive. *Requires TMUA.*  

**UCL – Computer Science BSc and Robotics & AI MEng:** UCL (London) offers a *BSc Computer Science* (G400) and a *MEng in Robotics & Artificial Intelligence* (H700)【90†L129-L137】【92†L159-L168】. Research groups include the **UCL Centre for Artificial Intelligence** and the **UCL Robotics Institute**【95†L89-L98】【96†L44-L52】. UCL also runs interdisciplinary AI labs (e.g. Urban AI). No separate pure AI undergrad degree (the Robotics & AI MEng is nearest). Entry is A*AA (with A* in Math) for BSc【90†L129-L137】, and A*A*A (A* Math) for the MEng【92†L159-L168】. UCL does *not* require TMUA/MAT/STEP for CS. Indian entry typically needs ~90% in CBSE/ISC. Acceptance is moderate (~15-25%). International tuition is **£43,500–£46,700**/yr【90†L216-L218】【92†L124-L132】. UCL offers substantial global scholarships: e.g. the **UCL Global Undergraduate Scholarships** (33 awards up to full fee, including 3 for India)【99†L307-L315】.  

**University of Manchester – Computer Science BSc:** Manchester’s *BSc Computer Science* (with honors) is strong in AI. It hosts a **Centre for Robotics and AI** focusing on machine learning and autonomous systems【106†L39-L48】, plus ML and NLP research groups. No dedicated AI-only degree (students may take AI/ML modules). Undergrads can engage in faculty-led research and a project. Entry is A*A*A (A* in Maths plus science)【101†L387-L395】; no external test needed. For Indian applicants, Manchester typically expects CBSE/ISC ~90% overall (≈90% in Math, 85% in Physics/CS)【104†L363-L370】. Fees for internationals are about **£37,800**/yr【108†L6-L10】. Manchester offers generous scholarships: the *Global Futures* scheme awards >100 undergrad scholarships (up to £30k total, £10k/year) to top international students (including from South Asia)【110†L454-L463】.  

**University of Bristol – BSc Computer Science (with AI):** Bristol offers a *BSc in Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence* (G405)【113†L127-L132】 (and a plain CS BSc). The world‐renowned **Bristol Robotics Lab (BRL)** is a joint UW–UWE facility【114†L0-L4】, focusing on robotics, machine learning and AI. The department’s Isambard AI supercomputer also supports AI research. Students undertake team projects and a final-year research project. Entry is A*AA (A* in Maths)【113†L151-L158】. Indian entry typically requires ~90% (CBSE ≈90% for equivalent A*AA)【117†L134-L138】. Fees are around **£33,400**/yr【113†L130-L133】. Bristol offers some international scholarships (e.g. *Think Big* awards)【117†L192-L200】 but few full-ride scholarships. *No TMUA/MAT/STEP required.*

### Top Canadian Universities (beyond Waterloo)  

**University of Toronto – Computer Science (BSc Specialist/Major):** UofT’s CS program (at St. George campus) is top-ranked in Canada. It offers **Computer Science Specialist and Major** streams; also CS in Engineering (ECE). Toronto is closely tied to the **Vector Institute** for AI and CIFAR AI group. CS faculty work in AI/ML subfields (robotics, computer vision, NLP, knowledge representation)【125†L151-L162】. Undergraduate research is encouraged (directed studies, project courses, reading groups)【147†L192-L200】. Entry requires strong grades (e.g. IB≈40+, 95%+ in top boards) plus Math 12; UofT admits Indian students with CBSE/ISC credentials (Class XII including Math)【135†L180-L189】. Approx. **CAD \$60–63K**/yr international tuition for CS. Scholarships include the *Lester B. Pearson* award (full) and several admission scholarships, but UofT has few India-specific awards.  

**University of British Columbia – Computer Science BSc:** UBC (Vancouver) offers a *BSc in Computer Science* (honours, co-op possible)【147†L134-L142】. Its **Institute for Computing, Information & Cognitive Systems (ICICS)** hosts AI research, and UBC has active labs in ML, NLP, and an interdisciplinary **Robotics group**. Undergrads may join research groups and specialized reading seminars【147†L192-L200】. Entry typically requires ~90%+ in high school (A in Mathematics), IB 40+; UBC accepts CBSE/ISC. International tuition for CS is about **CAD \$53,000**/yr【156†L231-L239】. UBC awards generous scholarships: e.g. the *International Scholars Program* provides full tuition & support to outstanding international admits【157†L143-L152】.  

**McGill University – Computer Science (BSc Honours/Major):** McGill (Montreal) has a BSc in Computer Science (Honours/Major)【163†L33-L41】, including a formal *Major in Artificial Intelligence* track. McGill houses the **Centre for Intelligent Machines (CIM)**【161†L44-L52】, a world-leading lab in robotics and AI (vision, control, etc). Other AI research includes groups in machine learning and NLP. Students do co-op placements and final-year research projects. Entry for Indians typically requires very high Class XII marks plus prerequisites (Math, Physics). International tuition is roughly **CAD \$19,000–20,000**/yr (Quebec $ + supplement); McGill guarantees fixed rate for international students【168†L148-L157】. Scholarships: *Major Entrance Scholarships* (CAD \$20K/yr renewable) and others are available but very competitive.  

**University of Alberta – Computer Science BSc:** UAlberta (Edmonton) offers BSc CS (with specializations and an option in AI). The famous **Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute (Amii)** is based here. Its CS department runs labs in machine learning, robotics and vision. Undergrads do honors projects and can engage in Amii summer research. Admission needs ~85–90% in top-level courses (A1 grades CBSE/ISC). International tuition is about **CAD \$35–46K**/yr【178†L11-L14】. UAlberta offers entrance scholarships (e.g. 4‑yr *International Admission Scholarship*) up to CAD \$20K total.  

**Simon Fraser University – Computing Science BSc/BA:** SFU’s Computing Science is strong in AI and robotics. It offers BSc (or BA) in Computing Science【170†L148-L157】. Key labs include the **Autonomous Intelligence and Robotics (AIRob) Lab** and a large **Machine Learning Group**【171†L1-L4】【171†L9-L11】. Students can do co-op and research projects. Entry requires about 75%+ in Canadian courses (for international, equivalent high marks). International tuition is ~**CAD \$30–40K**. Scholarships: SFU has entrance scholarships (e.g. *President’s Entrance Award* up to \$3K, many smaller awards) for high-achieving internationals.  

**University of Ottawa – Software Engineering & Computer Science BEng/BSc:** (If covered) Ottawa offers BSc/BA in Computer Science and a well-regarded co-op Software Engineering degree. Research groups include AI, data science, and the **EPICentre** for AI innovation. International tuition is ≈CAD \$30–38K. Entrance scholarships (President’s, Chancellor’s up to \$30K) are offered to top intl admits.  

**McMaster University – Computer Science BSc:** (If covered) McMaster has a BSc Computer Science (Honours) with AI modules. It participates in **MILA** (Quebec AI Institute) and has its own ML research group. Co-op and research projects available. Intl tuition ~CAD \$35–38K. Scholarships: McMaster *Faculty of Engineering Scholarships* (~\$6K–10K) for high-average internationals.  

**Note:** Cambridge, Oxford, Imperial all **require an admissions test** (TMUA or MAT) for CS entry【16†L583-L591】【41†L137-L145】【72†L27-L32】. Other UK universities listed do *not* require extra tests.
