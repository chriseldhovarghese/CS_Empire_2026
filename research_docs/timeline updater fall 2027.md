# Application Timeline Overview  
Across top CS/AI undergrad programs globally, admissions fall into three key windows.  In the **USA**, most elite schools hold Early Decision/Action (ED/EA) deadlines in early November and Regular Decision in January.  For example, many Ivy League and Tech schools set ED around Nov 1【44†L119-L127】.  Some state-university EAs fall earlier: Georgia Tech, UNC, UGA and UT–Austin use Oct 15 deadlines【45†L327-L330】.  Regular Decision offers for most U.S. colleges are typically released by late March.  

In **Canada**, leading universities open applications in early fall and close by year-end.  University of Waterloo’s deadlines (for Sept 2026 entry) were Jan 15 (Engineering) or Jan 30 (others) with documents due shortly after【50†L171-L179】.  University of Toronto Arts & Science typically has a Jan 15 deadline, though it extended the Fall 2026 CS application to Feb 2, 2026【60†L1-L9】.  The University of British Columbia has staggered dates: UBC’s International Scholars application closes Jan 15 (2026), but its internal scholarship application deadline was Nov 17, 2025 for Fall 2026 (to support a Sept 2026 start)【5†L174-L182】.  Many Canadian offers are finally issued by late winter.  

The **UK** follows UCAS rules: applications for Oxbridge and most medicine/dentistry/vet courses are due Oct 15 of the preceding year【48†L671-L679】.  Most other programs use a Jan deadline (e.g. Jan 13, 2027 for Sept 2027 entry)【48†L671-L679】.  Decisions typically arrive by May.  The UK also offers international scholarships: e.g. the UK Global Futures program invites eligible North American applicants (who applied by Jan 14) to submit a scholarship form by early March【22†L523-L531】.  

In **Singapore**, NUS and NTU have their own cycle.  For admission to AY2026/27, NUS applications ran roughly Dec 17, 2025–Mar 19, 2026 (varies by qualification)【64†L275-L279】.  Admitted Singapore students often attend post-offer “Discovery Weeks” or interviews in Mar–Apr.  Successful applicants from overseas may be offered the government’s MOE Tuition Grant; at NTU, for example, those offered admission with the TG had to accept by mid-July (July 18, 2025 for the 2026 intake)【13†L804-L812】.  

Australian institutions set deadlines in the Southern Hemisphere summer.  ANU’s deadlines for Semester 1 2027 closed Dec 15, 2026 (with some courses by Oct 31, 2026)【70†L495-L501】.  The University of Melbourne required applications by Oct 31, 2026 for its Feb 2027 intake【68†L9-L10】.  Applicants generally receive offers by January.  

European CS/AI programs vary by country.  In **Continental Europe**, for example, ETH Zurich required international applicants to apply by late November (1–30 Nov 2025 for 2026 entry)【81†L61-L70】.  EPFL (Lausanne) uses an admission exam for oversubscribed programs: applicants filled an online form in spring, took a math entrance test in May, and heard results by July【74†L141-L149】.  German universities typically require the TestAS: for 2026 intake, TestAS was offered Mar 19, Apr 25 and Jun 9, 2026【34†L195-L202】 (with more sessions later).  

## Key Deadlines by Region  
- **Oct 15 (UK/US):** By Oct 15, UK aspirants must have UCAS entries for Oxbridge and medicine/dentistry【48†L671-L679】.  In the US, a few EA programs (e.g. Georgia Tech, UT–Austin, UGA) also fall on Oct 15【45†L327-L330】.  
- **Nov 1 (US):** The majority of US ED/REA deadlines are Nov 1【44†L119-L127】 (Ivies, MIT, etc.), with decisions out by mid-Dec.  No similar deadline exists in Canada; U.S. Regular Decision and most Canadian offers come in Jan–Mar.  
- **Dec 1 (Canada):** Canadian universities emphasize Dec 1 as a priority date.  For instance, UBC international applicants had to apply by Dec 1 to be considered for scholarships【5†L174-L182】. (UBC’s scholarship deadlines fell Nov 17 for 2026 entry.)  Most other Canadian UG deadlines are Jan 15 (Ontario) or Jan 31 in Western Canada.  

## International Scholarships & Aid  
Top programs often run separate deadlines for scholarships.  At U of T, the *Lester B. Pearson* scholarship requires school nomination by early Oct and student OUAC application by mid-Oct【82†L262-L270】, with the Pearson application due early Nov【82†L262-L270】.  UBC’s International Scholars awards similarly expect a mid-Nov application (for Fall 2027 intake, likely Nov 2026) and a Dec 1 university application【5†L174-L182】.  NUS/NTU Singapore have merit awards: international admits often accept the MOE Tuition Grant by July (NTU set July 18 for the 2026 cohort)【13†L804-L812】.  UK Global Futures scholarships require early planning: e.g. Manchester’s Global Futures invited North American applicants (with Jan UCAS) to apply for the scholarship by Mar 5【22†L523-L531】.  

## Admission Tests & Exams  
Applicants must book required tests well ahead of deadlines.  The **SAT** runs multiple dates: for 2025–26, tests were offered Oct, Nov, Dec 2025 and Mar–June 2026【24†L152-L160】.  Fall 2027 applicants typically take SAT by late 2026.  British math tests: Cambridge’s *TMUA* is offered in Oct and Jan each year; Cambridge College requires the Oct sitting【28†L133-L137】.  Oxford (which phased out MAT) also uses TMUA as of 2026【32†L75-L83】.  The STEP exam for Cambridge/UCL is held in June (for 2026 entry, Step 2 on June 4 and Step 3 on June 10, 2026【79†L213-L223】), with registrations closing in early May.  Germany’s TestAS had sessions in March, April, June 2026【34†L195-L202】 (and October 2026), with registration typically 4–6 weeks prior.  

## Supplemental Applications  
Several programs require extra forms or interviews.  U of T’s CS major (Arts & Science) mandates a supplementary “CS Profile” after the OUAC application.  For Fall 2026, Arts & Science extended CS’s application to Feb 2, 2026【60†L1-L9】 and alerted applicants to submit their supplemental profiles after applying.  Waterloo’s “Admission Information Form” (AIF) must be completed post-application; Waterloo opened courses Jan 30, 2026 and required all supplementary docs by Feb 13, 2026【50†L171-L179】.  In Singapore, applicants invited to NUS or NTU interviews (Discovery Week) must attend in March/April following their application【64†L275-L279】.  

Overall, US programs cluster around Nov 1 for ED, Canadian programs cluster around Dec 1–Jan 15, and UK (Oxbridge/Med) use Oct 15.  International scholarships and tests add their own rhythms.  Applicants should align each school’s specific deadlines (as cited above) with these milestones【44†L119-L127】【48†L671-L679】【5†L174-L182】【82†L262-L270】 to maximize opportunities.
