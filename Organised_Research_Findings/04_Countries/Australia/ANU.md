# Australian National University (ANU)

> **Location:** Canberra, ACT, Australia
> **Track:** Dual (Fall 2026 Sem 2: May 15 / Fall 2027 Feb Intake: Dec 15, 2026)
> **Program:** BAdvComp (Honours) / BAdvComp (R&D Honours) — 192 units

---

## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### AI & Human-Centred Computing
- Profoundly deep specializations in **Artificial Intelligence** and **Human-Centred and Creative Computing**.
- Small, highly interactive classes taught by global experts.
- Unparalleled ratio of Nobel laureates among staff and alumni.

### Research-First Architecture
- **BAdvComp (R&D Honours)** — Embeds advanced research from **semester 1 of year 1**.
- Not a bolt-on thesis year — the entire degree IS a research vehicle.
- Specifically calibrated to produce premier candidates for the world's leading PhD programs.

## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **R&D Honours is the crown jewel** — the ONLY Australian program that embeds PhD-prep research from Day 1. But the 95% CBSE / 1430 SAT bar is steep.
2. **Two tiers of the same degree:** Standard Honours (81% entry) vs R&D Honours (95% entry). Aim for R&D.
3. **Canberra location** — proximity to national defense, intelligence, and policy institutions.
4. **Specialist Math prerequisite is non-negotiable** — ensure Indian curriculum covers equivalent content.
5. ANU produces the **highest proportion of PhD-track graduates** in Australia.
