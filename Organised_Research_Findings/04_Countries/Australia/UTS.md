# University of Technology Sydney (UTS)

> **Location:** Sydney CBD, NSW, Australia
> **Track:** Dual (Fall 2026 Spring: Apr 30 / Fall 2027 Feb Intake: Oct-Dec 2026)
> **Program:** BCompSc (Honours, 4yr, 192cp) / **B Artificial Intelligence** (NEW)

---

## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### AI — Dedicated AI Bachelor's Degree
- Newly launched **Bachelor of Artificial Intelligence** — rare at the undergraduate level in Australia.
- BCompSc (Honours) is exhaustive: complex mathematics, advanced algorithms, and systems design.
- Ranked **Top 100 globally** in CS.

### Research-Embedded Curriculum
- Research is **hardcoded into the degree** — not extracurricular.
- Mandatory modules: Technology Research Preparation → Technology Research Methods → **24-credit-point Honours Project**.
- Honours structure mandates substantial research alongside leading UTS researchers.
