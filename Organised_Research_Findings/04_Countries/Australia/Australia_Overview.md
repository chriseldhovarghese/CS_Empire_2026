## 🎯 Exam Relevance (Go8 Universities)

### SAT (🟢 Direct Entry Bypass — Highest Structural Value Globally)
Australian universities use SAT scores **mechanically** with published thresholds. A 1550+ far exceeds all requirements and **bypasses the mandatory foundation year entirely**.

| Australian University | Min SAT for Direct Entry | 1550+ Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **U-Melbourne (Science)** | **1320** | 🟢 Guaranteed direct entry, far above threshold |
| **U-Melbourne (Biomedicine/Commerce)** | **1400** | 🟢 Guaranteed direct entry |
| **U-Sydney** | ~**1300–1400** (program dependent) | 🟢 Guaranteed direct entry |
| **UNSW** | ~**1280–1380** | 🟢 Guaranteed direct entry |

- **Key Insight:** Australia is the **safest SAT-based pathway** globally. The admissions model is threshold-based, not holistic. Once you meet the score, you're in.
- AP scores (4–5) combined with SAT can unlock even more pathways and potentially grant course credit.

### JEE (⚪ Minimal Impact)
- Neither JEE Mains nor Advanced is structurally recognized by Australian universities as a bypass mechanism.
- At best, JEE is mentioned in application narratives as a supplementary "rigor signal." It has no formal admissions weight.

## 🔴 Policy Traps & Hostility Signals
1. **Course-Hopping Block:** Strict rules against "downgrading" from Uni to cheaper VET courses.
2. **The "Genuine Student" (GS) Test:** Replaced GTE. Requires a bespoke statement linking prior education to future career in India.
3. **Points-Test Disconnect:** Onshore STEM experience only yields 5 points, same as a "Professional Year."
