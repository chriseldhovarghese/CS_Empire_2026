## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### Artificial Intelligence — Dedicated Degree
- **School of Informatics** — One of Europe's largest and most research-intensive CS departments.
- Dedicated undergraduate and postgraduate AI degrees with integrated work placements and sponsorship schemes.

### Research Institutes Accessible to Undergrads
| Institute | Focus |
|:----------|:------|
| **IPAB (Perception, Action & Behaviour)** | Insect robotics, surgical robotics, cognitive robot systems |
| **Machine Intelligence Group** | Statistical learning, deep generative models |
| **CISA (Centre for Intelligent Systems)** | Multi-agent systems, knowledge representation |

### Paid Undergraduate Research
- **Informatics Undergraduate Research Experience Scheme** — Short-term employment contract at **£2,060.75/month** for up to 8 weeks during summer.
- This is **paid employment, not a stipend** — students earn a competitive salary while conducting real AI research.

## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **Dedicated AI degree** (not just CS with AI track) — rare at this caliber globally.
2. **Paid research experience** (£2,060/month) is uniquely structured as employment — superior to unpaid UROP models.
3. **Offer rate volatility** (33% to 53%) means timing and cohort competition matter.
4. Edinburgh's **UCAS cap of 5 choices** means this selection competes with Oxford/Cambridge/Imperial/UCL slots.
5. Scotland's 4-year undergraduate model (vs England's 3-year) provides more depth and research time.
