## 🗺️ Application Roadmap & Narrative (2027)

### The Strategy: "The Pure Logic Play"
- **UCAS Deadline (Oct 15):** Non-negotiable. 
- **The Test (TMUA):** The single most important filter. Oxford moved from MAT to TMUA for CS. An 8.0+ practically guarantees an interview; a 6.0 is the danger zone.
- **Narrative Hook:** "The Mathematical Computer Scientist." Oxford views CS as a branch of Mathematics. Your Personal Statement (PS) should be 80% super-curricular (math logic, algorithms, reading beyond the syllabus).

### Critical Deadlines
- **Sept 2026:** UCAS Open.
- **Oct 15, 2026:** Application Deadline.
- **Late Oct 2026:** TMUA Test Window.
- **Dec 2026:** Interview Week (Intense technical grilling).

## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **£62,820/yr** = the single most expensive option in the entire Empire portfolio.
2. **7% acceptance** with no real financial aid = extreme risk/cost ratio.
3. **TMUA is mandatory** (same as Cambridge) — one test preps for both.
4. **Further Mathematics A\*** is a hard prerequisite — must have equivalent curriculum preparation.
5. **Cannot apply to both Oxford and Cambridge via UCAS** — must choose one.
6. **ORI is world-class** — worth it only if robotics is the primary ambition AND finances are not a constraint.
