## 🗺️ Application Roadmap & Narrative (2027)

### The Strategy: "The Algorithmic Purist"
- **TMUA Baseline:** Aim for a "top decile" score. 
- **Narrative Hook:** "The Problem Solver." Your PS should detail your journey through competitive programming (USACO/IOI) or advanced mathematical concepts (Graph Theory, Combinatorics).
- **Collateral: The CSAT (Computer Science Admissions Test):** Be aware that some colleges (e.g., Trinity) may set additional internal assessments during the interview.

- **Oct 15, 2026:** Application Deadline.

## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **CBSE alone does NOT qualify for Cambridge CS.** You need AP/IB/A-Level on top.
2. **TMUA is mandatory** — must prepare specifically for this aptitude test.
3. **DeepMind Research Ready** internship = direct Google DeepMind pipeline.
4. **£44K/yr** with limited scholarships = one of the most expensive options globally.
5. Competes with Oxford for UCAS slots (5 total — cannot apply to both Oxbridge).
