# Nagoya University — G30 International Programs

> **Location:** Nagoya, Japan (industrial heartland)
> **Category:** 🏆 HIDDEN GEM — Automotive AI & Robotics (Toyota Ecosystem)
> **Program:** BSc in Automotive Engineering (Mechanical / Electrical-Electronic-Information tracks)

---

## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### Automotive AI & CASE Technologies
- Focus on **CASE (Connected, Autonomous, Shared, Electric)** technologies.
- Bridges traditional mechanical design with signal processing, software engineering, and power electronics.
- **Unique:** "Introduction to Automotive Engineering" course — instructors from **Toyota and Denso** guide physical vehicle disassembly/assembly.
- From **Year 3**, focus shifts entirely to project-based learning in specialized labs.

### Industry Integration
- Located in Japan's automotive manufacturing hub — surrounded by Toyota, Denso, Aisin, and advanced manufacturers.
- Students get unprecedented access to corporate mentorship and internship pipelines.
