# TU Delft (Delft University of Technology)

> **Location:** Delft, Netherlands
> **Category:** Peak European Engineering
> **Program:** BSc in Computer Science & Engineering (English-taught track)

---

## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### AI — 24 Dedicated Delft AI Labs
- AI is treated as a **ubiquitous tool across all engineering faculties**, not isolated software.
- **24 "Delft AI Labs"** bridge AI foundations with societal/scientific challenges:
  - **3DUU Lab** — 3D spatial urban modeling
  - **AIFluids Lab** — AI merged with fluid mechanics for aerospace
  - **Biomorphic Intelligence Lab** — Bio-inspired algorithms for drone swarms
- **CoR (Dept. of Cognitive Robotics)** — RoboHouse and SAM|XL field labs for human-robot interaction and intelligent vehicle control.

## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **You CANNOT apply to TU Delft from Indian 12th grade.** You need 1 year of Indian university first.
2. The 50% acceptance rate is a **trap** — the real selection happens in Year 1 (25-40% fail out).
3. **BSA failure = expelled AND visa revoked** — extremely high-stakes.
4. **24 AI Labs** and the **Biomorphic Intelligence Lab** are genuinely world-class for drone/swarm robotics.
5. **Strategy:** Apply after completing Year 1 of Indian BTech/BSc. This means TU Delft is a **Year 2 transfer option**, not a fresh application.
