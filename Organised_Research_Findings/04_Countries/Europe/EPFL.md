# EPFL (École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne)

> **Location:** Lausanne, Switzerland
> **Category:** Peak European STEM (paired with ETH Zurich)
> **Program:** BSc in Computer Science (French-taught primarily) / Summer Research

---

## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### Quantum Computing
- **CQSL (Computational Quantum Science Lab)** — Led by **Giuseppe Carleo**. Develops ML techniques to simulate condensed matter and electronic structures for NISQ (Noisy Intermediate-Scale Quantum) devices.

### Robotics
- **Biorobotics Laboratory** — Soft transducers, swarm robotics.
- **Reconfigurable Robotics Lab** — Distributed intelligent systems, computational robot design.
