## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### Robotics — Defense-Grade Infrastructure
| Lab | Director | Focus |
|:----|:---------|:------|
| **ANPL (Autonomous Navigation & Perception Lab)** | Vadim Indelman | SLAM in GPS-deprived environments, belief space planning for drone swarms |
| **CRL (Computational Robotics Lab)** | — | Steerable medical needles, automated warehouse planning |
| **MRS Lab (Multi-Robot Systems)** | Kiril Solovey | Kinodynamic planning, **F1TENTH autonomous racecar team** |

- **F1TENTH** = undergrads build and race autonomous cars head-to-head — immediate, high-stakes hardware experience.
- Labs are deeply intertwined with **Israeli defense sector** and global tech firms.

### AI & Cognition — Unique Minor
- **Minor in Cognition** — Links cognitive psychology, game theory, and AI. Designed for engineers who want to build brain-tech and human-machine interaction systems.
- This minor is **unique in the global landscape** — no other target school offers a formal cognitive science + engineering integration at the undergrad level.
