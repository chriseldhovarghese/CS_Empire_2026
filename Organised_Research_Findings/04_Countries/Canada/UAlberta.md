# University of Alberta — Computer Science

> **Location:** Edmonton, Alberta, Canada
> **Category:** 🏆 HIDDEN GEM — Home of Amii (Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute)
> **Program:** BSc Computer Science (with AI specialization option)

---

## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### AI & Machine Learning — Amii Ecosystem
- **Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute (Amii)** — one of Canada's three national AI institutes (alongside Vector and MILA).
- **Richard Sutton** (Reinforcement Learning pioneer) — foundational RL research.
- Labs in ML, robotics, computer vision.
- Amii summer research opportunities for undergrads.

## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **Amii = RL + deep learning epicenter.** Sutton's lab is the birthplace of modern reinforcement learning.
2. **CAD $35-46K** range is competitive but not the cheapest (McGill is much cheaper).
3. **Edmonton location** — less tech-industry-dense than Toronto/Vancouver but strong in energy AI and resource optimization.
4. **Strong target/safety** for AI-focused applicants who want proximity to foundational RL research.
