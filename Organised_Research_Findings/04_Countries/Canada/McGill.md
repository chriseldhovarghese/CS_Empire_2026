## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **CAD $19-20K/yr** = **cheapest CS degree in Canada AND cheaper than most European options.** This is extraordinary.
2. **Fixed tuition guarantee** = no annual fee increases. Financial predictability.
3. **Formal AI Major** — not just a track or elective, but a named programme.
4. **CIM** is a globally recognized robotics/vision lab.
5. **Montreal ecosystem** — MILA (Quebec AI Institute), Yoshua Bengio's group, Element AI heritage.
6. **Quebec** means French is predominant outside campus — not a barrier but worth noting.
