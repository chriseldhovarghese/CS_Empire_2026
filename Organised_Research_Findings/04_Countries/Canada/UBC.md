# University of British Columbia (UBC)

> **Location:** Vancouver, BC, Canada
> **Track:** Fall 2027 (Award Priority: Dec 1 / Regular: Jan 15)
> **Status:** Fall 2026 Closed
> **Program:** BSc in Computer Science (Honours, Co-op available)

---

## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### AI & Robotics
- **ICICS (Institute for Computing, Information & Cognitive Systems)** — AI research hub.
- Active labs in ML, NLP, and interdisciplinary **Robotics group**.
- Co-op placements with Vancouver tech companies (Amazon, Microsoft, SAP).

## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **International Scholars Program** = one of the few full-ride options in Canada. Highly competitive.
2. **Vancouver location** = Pacific tech hub (Amazon, Microsoft). Strong co-op pipeline.
3. Co-op is **available but not mandatory** — unlike Waterloo's enforced model.
4. **ICICS** is strong but UBC is more of a general-strength CS school than a deep-niche specialist.
