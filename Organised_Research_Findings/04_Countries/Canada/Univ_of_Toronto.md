## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **Vector Institute / CIFAR** = the deepest academic AI ecosystem in Canada.
2. **Hinton's legacy** puts UofT permanently on any ML researcher's map.
3. **CAD $60-63K/yr** is prohibitively expensive — Pearson is near-impossible.
4. **CS PoSt (Program of Study)** system: students must earn competitive grades in first-year courses to declare the CS major — NOT guaranteed upon admission.
5. **Three campuses** — St. George is the elite one; Scarborough/Mississauga are different.
