# University of Washington (Seattle)

> **Location:** Seattle, Washington, USA
> **Track:** Fall 2026 (if rolling) / Fall 2027
> **Program:** BS in Computer Science (Paul G. Allen School of CSE)

---

## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### Artificial Intelligence — "AI for Engineering" Focus
- **AIMS Lab (AI for bioMedical Sciences)** — Explainable AI applied to clinical and biomedical contexts.
- **UbiComp Lab (Ubiquitous Computing)** — Edge computing, wearable sensing, context-aware systems.
- **Standout Faculty:**
  - **Su-In Lee** — Explainable AI, model auditing in clinical decision-making
  - **Sara Mostafavi** — Computational genomics, disease driver identification
  - **Vikram Iyer** — ML optimization integrated directly into hardware design; battery-free robotics
- **IMDS (Institute for Medical Data Science)** — Joint venture between Allen School, School of Medicine, and School of Public Health. Undergrads access cutting-edge medical datasets for algorithmic training.

### Robotics — The Allen School Powerhouse
UW Seattle has **the densest concentration of robotics labs** among our target universities:

| Lab | Director | Focus |
|:----|:---------|:------|
| **Robot Learning Lab** | Dieter Fox | Embodied AI, ML perception, manipulation under uncertainty |
| **Human-Centered Robotics Lab** | — | Safe human-robot co-existence |
| **Personal Robotics Lab** | — | Assistive robotics, everyday manipulation |
| **UbiComp / Iyer Group** | Vikram Iyer | Battery-free insect-scale robotics, bio-inspired edge sensors |

- **Key Insight:** Vikram Iyer's work on **battery-free, insect-scale robots** with bio-inspired sensors is genuinely unique globally. No other target university offers this niche.

### Bio-Computation
- Through **IMDS** and the **AIMS Lab**, UW provides strong CS + biomedical convergence.
- Less CRISPR-focused than MIT/Berkeley but strong on clinical AI deployment and model auditing.

## 🎓 Undergraduate Research Architecture

| Program | Structure | Compensation | Access Level |
|:--------|:----------|:-------------|:-------------|
| **Allen School URAs** | Term-based | Stipend/credit | Faculty-arranged, accessible to strong sophomores+ |
| **DUB (Design, Use, Build) Group** | Interdisciplinary | Varies | UX/HCI research with CS integration |

- UW's model is **interdisciplinary by design** — the IMDS joint structure means undergrads routinely cross school boundaries.
- **Industry pipeline:** Amazon, Microsoft, Boeing HQs are in Seattle — internship access is unmatched for robotics/AI.

## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **Direct-to-Major vs. Pre-Major:** Allen School CS has limited capacity. Applicants can be admitted to UW but NOT to the CS major directly. Pre-major students must reapply internally — risky.
2. **The robotics cluster** (Fox + Iyer + Human-Centered) makes UW the **#1 target for robotics-focused applicants** across our entire list.
3. **Seattle industry ecosystem** (Amazon Robotics, Microsoft Research, Boeing) provides internship density that Stanford/MIT can't match for hardware-integrated AI.
4. **Cost advantage:** As a public university, OOS tuition is significantly lower than private T20s.
