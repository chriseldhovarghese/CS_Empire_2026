## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### AI & Machine Learning
- **BAIR (Berkeley AI Research Lab)** — Immersive undergraduate access to reinforcement learning, autonomous agents, NLP, and computer vision.
- Energized by geographic proximity to **Silicon Valley** — Google DeepMind, Meta FAIR, OpenAI are all within commuting distance.

### Silicon / Chip Design — Undergraduate Tape-Out Revolution
- **Professor Borivoje Nikolić** — Directed a class where **19 undergrads with zero prior chip experience** completed a full chip tape-out in a single semester.
- **Key Insight:** Berkeley is redefining the entry parameters for hardware engineering by proving undergrads can execute complex circuit designs rapidly.

### Bio-Computation — Birthplace of CRISPR
- **Innovative Genomics Institute (IGI)** — Founded by **Jennifer Doudna** (Nobel Prize, CRISPR co-discoverer).
- **CCTD (Center for CRISPR Target Discovery)** — Pooled CRISPR functional genomic screening, NGS data processing.
- Undergrads with computational backgrounds are critical for the bioinformatic pipeline (trimming, gRNA mapping, abundance analysis).

### Cybersecurity & AI Safety
- **SUPERB AI4E (Summer Undergrad Program in Engineering Research — AI for Engineering)** — 9-week program studying limitations, adversarial vulnerabilities, and safety of ML algorithms.

## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **L&S CS vs EECS:** Two paths to CS at Berkeley. EECS (College of Engineering) = direct admit. L&S CS = declare the major after arrival. L&S is a **backup route** if EECS rejects.
2. **CRISPR birthplace + BAIR** = the only school rivaling MIT for CS + Bio + AI convergence.
3. **Tape-out class** makes Berkeley the #1 target for hardware-focused CS students.
4. UC application deadline is **Nov 30** (earlier than most privates).
