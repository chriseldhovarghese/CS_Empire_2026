## 🎓 Undergraduate Research Architecture

| Program | Structure | Compensation | Access Level |
|:--------|:----------|:-------------|:-------------|
| **IWSP (Integrated Work Study Programme)** | **8 months** industry immersion | Industry salary | Mandatory — replaces academic terms |
| **ITP (Integrative Team Project)** | Capstone | Academic credit | Final year |

- **IWSP is 8 months** — the longest mandatory industry placement in Singapore.
- Students frequently receive **full-time employment offers from IWSP hosts**.

## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **Applied AI + MLOps** focus = graduates deploy production AI systems on Day 1. Uniquely practical.
2. **8-month IWSP** is the most immersive industry placement across Singapore — strong for immediate employment.
3. **Credit-based pricing** makes cost variable — plan unit loads carefully.
4. **DigiPen joint degree** is expensive (SGD $165K total) but unique for game/simulation engineering.
5. SIT is the **most vocational** of Singapore's universities — optimized for industry, not academia.
