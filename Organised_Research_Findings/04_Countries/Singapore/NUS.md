## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
- **School of Computing** — Structured undergraduate tracks in AI and data analytics.
- Collaboration with **Microsoft Research Asia** on ML applications (NLP, healthcare).
- **Key Faculty/Groups:**
  - **Boxiang Liu** — Integrates Deep Learning, NLP, and Computer Vision with gene expression mapping (Expression Quantitative Trait Loci / eQTL).

### Silicon / Chip Design — Neuromorphic Computing Pioneer
- **Mario Lanza** — Published in *Nature* demonstrating how standard silicon transistors can function as biological neurons and synapses — scalable, energy-efficient hardware-based neural networks.
- **SHINE Centre** — Fuses classical silicon design with neuromorphic engineering concepts.
- **Key Insight:** NUS is pioneering the **convergence of chip design and neuroscience** — a niche distinct from traditional VLSI.

### Bio-Computation
- **CRISPORE Lab** — Led by **Chunyi Hu**. Focus: high-resolution mechanism study of supermolecule complexes + CRISPR-Cas diagnostic platform innovation.
- **Boxiang Liu** (cross-listed) — Deep Learning architectures for mapping cis/trans gene regulators.

## 🎓 Undergraduate Research Architecture

| Program | Structure | Compensation | Access Level |
|:--------|:----------|:-------------|:-------------|
| **UROPS (Undergrad Research Opportunities in Science)** | Term-based | Stipend/credit | Broadly accessible |
| **REx (Research Experience)** | International deployment | Funded travel | Sponsors global lab placements |

- UROPS is modeled on MIT's UROP but with a **Science faculty focus**.
- REx can sponsor deployment to international partner labs — unique for broadening perspective.
