## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **£37,800/yr** = cheapest elite UK CS option by a significant margin.
2. **100+ Global Futures scholarships** = highest volume of international CS scholarships in UK.
3. **No TMUA/MAT** required — lower barrier than Oxbridge/Imperial.
4. **A\*A\*A** requirement is paradoxically higher than Cambridge (A\*A\*A vs A\*A\*A) — but no aptitude test offsets this.
5. Strong **cost-value ratio** — genuine quality at lower price point.
