## 🗺️ Application Roadmap & Narrative (2027)

### The Strategy: "The Applied Architect"
- **TMUA Performance:** mandatory filter. Aim for 7.0+.
- **Narrative Hook:** "The Builder." Unlike Oxford, Imperial loves implementation. Discuss projects where you used low-level languages (C/C++), hardware interfaces, or complex system architectures.
- **The Interview:** Conducted by faculty. Expect live coding or logic puzzles on a digital whiteboard.

### Critical Deadlines
- **Oct 15, 2026:** Priority Deadline (Recommended for Indian applicants due to high volume).
- **Jan 13, 2027:** Standard Deadline (Risky for CS).

## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **5% acceptance = more selective than Cambridge CS** (17%).
2. **TMUA mandatory** — same test as Oxbridge. Prepare once, apply to all three.
3. **Math & CS for AI BEng** is a unique degree not offered by Oxbridge or UCL.
4. **London location** = tech industry hub (Google, DeepMind, Meta London offices).
5. Under UCAS: Imperial competes for the same 5 slots as Oxbridge + Edinburgh + UCL.
