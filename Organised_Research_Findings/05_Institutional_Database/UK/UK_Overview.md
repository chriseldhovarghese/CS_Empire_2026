# UK Overview: Admission Strategy & Visa Reality (2026/2027)

---

## 🛂 Visa & Immigration Status (Student Visa)
- **Status Emoji:** 🟢 **High Grant Rate (**96%**) / 🔴 High Financial Boundary**
- **Financial Proof:** Maintenance funds (£1,334/month in London, £1,023/month outside) for 9 months + Tuition.
- **Work Rights:**
  - **In-Study:** 20 hrs/week (term time) / Full-time (holidays).
  - **Post-Study (Graduate Route):** **18–24 months** (subject to current policy reviews).
- **Settlement (Skilled Worker):** 
  - **Salary Floor:** £41,700 for most roles (new 2024/2025 baseline).
  - **The Dependant Ban:** International students (except Research/PhD) can no longer bring dependants.

## 🎯 Exam Relevance (Oxbridge, Imperial, UCL, Bristol)

### TMUA (🟢 Critical Path for Elite CS)
- **Mandatory for:** Cambridge CS, Oxford CS, Imperial CS.
- **Role:** High-stakes filter. MCQs focusing on pure logic and mathematical reasoning.
- **Prep:** 2-4 months. JEE students excel at the math but often struggle with the "logical deduction" syntax.

### Board Exams (🟢 The Foundation)
- **Equivalency:** CBSE/ISC 12th results are treated as A-Level equivalents.
- **Thresholds:** Elite offers are usually **Conditional** on 90–98% overall with 95%+ in Mathematics.

### IELTS / TOEFL (🟢 Mandatory)
- **Requirement:** 7.0–7.5 (overall) with no band below 6.5–7.0 for Oxbridge/Imperial.

| Tier | Exam Requirement | Competitive CS Range |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Oxbridge / Imperial** | TMUA + Board | TMUA 6.5+ / Board 95%+ |
| **UCL / Manchester** | Board Only | Board 92–95%+ |
| **Bristol / Edinburgh** | Board Only | Board 90%+ |

## 🔴 Policy Traps & Hostility Signals
1. **The Salary Floor Trap:** Graduating into a role that doesn't meet the £41,700 threshold, forcing an exit after the Graduate Route.
2. **Institutional Levy:** Proposed "Student Tax" (£925/yr) for the 2026 intake.
3. **NHS Surcharge:** Increased to £776/year (Immigration Health Surcharge - IHS).
