## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **No aptitude test required** = the ONLY top-5 London CS school with no TMUA/MAT. Major advantage.
2. **3 India-specific full-tuition scholarships** = the strongest UK financial aid pathway for Indian students.
3. **MEng Robotics & AI** is a dedicated 4-year integrated pathway — comparable to Edinburgh's AI degree.
4. **15-25% acceptance** is significantly more accessible than Imperial (5%) or Oxbridge (7-17%).
5. **Best UCAS slot** for students who want to avoid the TMUA preparation burden while still accessing London elite.
