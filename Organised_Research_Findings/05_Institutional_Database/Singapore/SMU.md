# Singapore Management University (SMU)

> **Location:** Singapore CBD
> **Track:** Fall 2027 (Jan-Mar 2027 window)
> **Status:** Fall 2026 Closed
 (Oct application opens)
> **Program:** BSc (Computer Science) — School of Computing & Information Systems (SCIS)

---

## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### AI/Cybersecurity — Business-Integrated CS
- **No traditional lectures** — entire curriculum is seminar-style, small class sizes.
- Highly applied tracks: **Frontier Artificial Intelligence**, **Cybersecurity**, **Software Systems**.
- Trains students to architect solutions understanding commercial, regulatory, and business contexts — not just code.
- Mandatory second major encouraged: AI + Business Analytics, Cybersecurity + IT Solution Management.

### Research Partnership
- **SMU-Carnegie Mellon Masters Fast Track** — direct pipeline to CMU graduate programs.

## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **CMU Fast Track** pipeline is a massive strategic advantage for grad school ambitions.
2. **NR Thadani Scholarship explicitly targets Indian nationals** — rare India-specific aid.
3. **Mandatory interview** filters for soft skills — prepare communication/leadership narratives.
4. **MOE Grant** effectively halves tuition but requires 3-year Singapore work commitment post-graduation.
5. **Business-CS fusion** makes SMU graduates ideal for consulting, fintech, and enterprise product management.
