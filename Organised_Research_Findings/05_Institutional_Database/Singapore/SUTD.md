## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### Design-Centric Engineering — Silo Destroyer
- Year 1 **"Freshmore"** common curriculum: all students get identical math, physics, and design foundations before specializing.
- Collaborative, project-based learning in cohorts of <40 students, led by professor + TA.
- **Hardware-software integration** focus: graduates are exceptionally competitive in physical product development, robotics, complex systems.
- **Design·AI architecture** — AI embedded as a design tool, not isolated software.

## 🎓 Undergraduate Research Architecture

| Program | Structure | Compensation | Access Level |
|:--------|:----------|:-------------|:-------------|
| **UROP** | Semester-based | **SGD $1,500** project funding + possible faculty grant funding | Any student not on academic probation |
| **SHARP (Honours & Research Programme)** | Elite track | Enhanced funding | Top-tier students only |

- **SGD $665 million+ in external research funding** secured since 2010.
- **83 startups incubated** — strong entrepreneurial pipeline.
- Students can publish in scientific journals directly from UROP projects.
