## 🛂 Visa & Immigration Status (Student's Pass)
- **Status Emoji:** 🟢 **Safe entry / 🔴 Hard PR**
- **Financial Proof:** Strictly tied to MOE/University declarations. Tuition SGD 20k–40k.
- **Work Rights:**
  - **Post-Study:** No automatic PGWP. Must secure an **Employment Pass (EP)** or **S Pass**.
  - **EP Floor (2026):** SGD 5,000+ per month for entry-level tech roles.
- **PR Pathway:** Highly discretionary. No guaranteed timeline; dependent on "contribution to Singapore" and demographics.

---

## 🎯 Exam Relevance (NUS, NTU)

### JEE Advanced (🟢 Direct Admission — Highest Value Outside India)
- Both NUS and NTU **officially list** "exceptional rank in top university entrance examinations (e.g. IIT/AIEEE rankings)" as a significant achievement.
- A **top 5,000 JEE Advanced rank** is treated as unimpeachable evidence of quantitative resilience for CS programs.
- JEE functions as a **substitute for or powerful supplement to** SAT Math in this context.
- **Board exam grades (CBSE/ISC) remain the primary baseline** — JEE does not replace them entirely.

### SAT (🟢 High Value — Benchmarking Tool)
- Min functional threshold for competitive CS applicants: **~1450**.
- A **1550+** solidifies the academic profile and is evaluated as evidence of stamina and meticulousness.
- Especially critical for applicants from non-traditional curricula (i.e., without A-Levels/IB diplomas).

### NUS/NTU Internal Assessments & Interviews
- Some programs require internal problem-solving assessments (logic, basic algorithms).
- Prep time: **1–3 months** if already doing JEE/Olympiad-level work.

| Exam | Value at NUS/NTU | Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **JEE Advanced (Top 5k)** | 🟢 **Highest** | Direct admit supplement, replaces SAT Math signal |
| **SAT 1550+** | 🟢 **High** | Benchmarking tool, especially for non-IB/A-Level students |
| **JEE Mains (99%+)** | 🟢 **Strong Signal** | Evidence of academic strength, weaker than Advanced |
| **IOI / Olympiads** | 🟢 **Elite** | Supreme differentiator for CS-specific admits |

## 🔴 Policy Traps & Hostility Signals
1. **The "Non-Settlement" Trap:** Treating Singapore as an immigration destination. It is a **career launchpad**, not a PR factory.
2. **Cost of Living:** One of the most expensive cities globally; housing costs can neutralize high salaries.
