## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### Quantum Computing
- **JARA Institute for Quantum Information** — Joint with Forschungszentrum Jülich.
- **Hendrik Bluhm** + **David DiVincenzo** — Semiconductor spin-qubits, Majorana qubits, quantum error correction.
- Dedicated lab course in quantum technology: photonic quantum interconnects for networking quantum computers across long distances.

### Silicon / Chip Design
- **ZMNT (Central Lab for Micro- and Nanotechnology)** — 1,054 sq meters of Class 5 cleanroom (doubling capacity underway).
- Focus: silicon photonics, MEMS, 2D material transistors.

4. Best strategy: Use UROP International for 10-week summer research → apply to RWTH MSc (English-taught) later.
