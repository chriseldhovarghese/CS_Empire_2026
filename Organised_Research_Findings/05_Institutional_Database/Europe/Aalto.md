## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **If you score 1540+ SAT, admission is effectively guaranteed** — regardless of Indian board performance.
2. This is the **purest meritocracy in global CS admissions** — zero subjectivity.
3. **1.6% acceptance rate** for Data Science = more selective than most Ivies, but with a **transparent, predictable filter.**
4. **Finnish tuition for non-EU students** costs ~€15,000-20,000/year — cheaper than US but not free.
5. **Quantum Technology BSc** is a rare offering — only Waterloo and Aalto offer this at the undergrad level.
6. The **SAT-only pathway** makes Aalto the **optimal "insurance policy"** for Indian students with 1540+ SAT but weak extracurriculars.
