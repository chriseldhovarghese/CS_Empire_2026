# Europe Overview: Germany, Netherlands, Sweden (2026)

---

> **Track:** Dual (Summer 2026: Apr 20 / Winter 2027: Oct-Jan 2027)
> **Status:** Fall 2026 Entry Rolling/Late Open.

## 🛂 Visa & Immigration Summary

### 🇩🇪 Germany: The ROI Leader
- **Status Emoji:** 🟢 **High Favorability**
- **Financial Proof:** **€11,208 Blocked Account (Sperrkonto)**.
- **Work Rights:** 18-month Job Seeker Visa post-graduation.
- **Fast-Track PR:** 21–33 months to PR with an EU Blue Card.
- **Nit-Pick:** Zero tuition at public unis is the biggest financial edge. B1/B2 German is non-negotiable for long-term career success.

### 🇳🇱 Netherlands: The Tech Hub
- **Status Emoji:** 🟡 **Moderate (Costly Living)**
- **Post-Study:** 12-month **Zoekjaar (Orientation Year)**.
- **Nit-Pick:** Zoekjaar doesn't count toward the 5-year PR residency. Tech salaries are high, but housing is at crisis levels.

### 🇸🇪 Sweden: The Innovation Base
- **Status Emoji:** 🟢 **Safe but Slower**
- **Post-Study:** 6–12 month Job Seeker.
- **Nit-Pick:** Weaker PR pipeline compared to Germany. Best for "EU experience" rather than immediate immigration guarantee.

## 🎯 Exam Relevance

### 🇩🇪 Germany: JEE Advanced → Studienkolleg Bypass (🟢 Critical Path)
Indian students with JEE Advanced qualification are **officially recognized as holding Abitur equivalence**. This completely bypasses the 1-year preparatory Studienkolleg.

| TU9 Institution | Preferred JEE Adv. Rank | Additional Requirements |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **TU Munich (TUM)** | Under **3,000** (Highly Competitive) | APS Certificate, German C1 (if German-taught) |
| **RWTH Aachen** | Under **4,000–7,000** | APS Certificate |
| **TU Berlin** | Flexible (qualification sufficient) | APS Certificate |
| **KIT (Karlsruhe)** | Flexible | APS Certificate |

> **⚠️ Mandatory:** All Indian applicants to Germany require an **APS (Academic Evaluation Centre) Certificate** (post-2023).

### 🇩🇪 Germany: TestAS (If No JEE)
- **Purpose:** Standard aptitude test for international non-EU students.
- **Difficulty:** Significantly below JEE. Pattern recognition & logical reasoning.
- **Prep Time:** **3–6 weeks** for a JEE-level student.
- **Strategic Value:** High percentile score strongly boosts English-taught CS program admission.

### 🇳🇱 Netherlands: TU Delft Numerus Fixus (🔴 SAT/JEE Irrelevant)
- TU Delft CS operates under capacity cap: **590 seats** for 2026–2027.
- Admission decided by **TU Delft's proprietary Matching & Selection cognitive tests** (algorithmic thinking, math, logical reasoning).
- **SAT and JEE are irrelevant here.** You must pass their internal assessment.

### 🇸🇪 Sweden: Board-Exam Driven
- SAT is supplementary at best. Swedish universities primarily evaluate Board exam equivalency (CBSE/ISC/IB).

### SAT / ACT Across Europe (🟡 Supplementary Only)
- SAT is not the central axis for European admissions. Board results (10+2) and country-specific tests (TestAS, TU Delft selection, ETH entrance exams) dominate.
- A 1550+ demonstrates aptitude but is secondary to the primary evaluation mechanisms.
