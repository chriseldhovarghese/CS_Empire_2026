# Simon Fraser University (SFU)

> **Location:** Burnaby/Surrey/Vancouver, BC, Canada
> **Program:** BSc/BA in Computing Science

---

## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### AI & Robotics
- **AIRob Lab (Autonomous Intelligence and Robotics)** — autonomous navigation, intelligent agents.
- **Large Machine Learning Group** — deep learning applications.
- Co-op program available.

## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **AIRob Lab** is a genuine robotics research center — not just a teaching lab.
2. **Most affordable Metro Vancouver option** — cheaper than UBC.
3. Lower prestige than UBC/Waterloo but strong co-op and industry connections.
4. **Good safety school** for Canadian portfolio diversification.
