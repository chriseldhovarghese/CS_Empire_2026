# University of Sydney

> **Location:** Sydney, NSW, Australia
> **Track:** Dual (Fall 2026 Sem 2: May 15 / Fall 2027 Feb Intake: Oct-Dec 2026)
> **Program:** Bachelor of Advanced Computing (4yr, 192 credit points)

---

## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### Cybersecurity — Strategic & Geopolitical Focus
- Not just technical security — curriculum cultivates **politically and socially-minded leaders** navigating corporate and geopolitical threat matrices.
- Majors: Computer Science, Cybersecurity, Software Development, Computational Data Science.

### Research Profile
- Ranked **38th globally** in CS (THE).
- Strong in software system design, human-computer interaction, and data-driven decision architectures.

## 🎓 Undergraduate Research Architecture

| Program | Structure | Compensation | Access Level |
|:--------|:----------|:-------------|:-------------|
| **Dalyell Scholars Program** | Full degree integration | AUD $3,000 Global Mobility Scholarship | **ATAR 98+ (≈95%+ CBSE) required for entry** |

- **Dalyell Scholars** get accelerated learning, early advanced unit access, bespoke mentoring.
- Embedded in team-based research: planetary health analysis, brain connectivity statistical modeling, mathematical modeling of human evolution.
- **Guaranteed** international research mobility funding.

## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **Dalyell Scholars** is the elite accelerator — but requires near-perfect marks to qualify.
2. **Cybersecurity major** is uniquely strategic/geopolitical in orientation — not just pentesting.
3. **4-year degree** (vs 3-year at UNSW) provides more depth and integrated Honours.
4. International scholarships limited — MySydney Scheme (AUD $8,500/yr) mostly targets domestic students.
