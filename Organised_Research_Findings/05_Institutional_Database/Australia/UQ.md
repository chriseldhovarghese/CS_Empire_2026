# University of Queensland (UQ)

> **Location:** Brisbane, QLD, Australia
> **Track:** Dual (Fall 2026 Sem 2: May 31 / Fall 2027 Feb Intake: Oct-Dec 2026)
> **Program:** Bachelor of Computer Science (3yr) / Honours (4yr)

---

## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### Applied & Interdisciplinary CS
- Strongest in **industry-ready software development**, systems analysis, and technology management.
- Seamless dual programs with Business Management, Commerce, Economics, Science, and Laws.
- Equipped to address complex, multidisciplinary global challenges upon graduation.

---

## 🎓 Undergraduate Research Architecture

| Program | Structure | Compensation | Access Level |
|:--------|:----------|:-------------|:-------------|
| **Honours Year** | 1-year full-time research sprint | Academic credit | Competitive — requires strong GPA |

- Research integration is **backend-loaded** — strongest in final-year Honours pathway.
- Less prominent early-stage research programs than UNSW or ANU.

## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. **Lowest entry threshold in Go8** (70% CBSE) — strong safety school.
2. Brisbane's **lower cost of living** (AUD $19K vs Sydney's $24K) improves overall affordability.
3. Dual-degree flexibility (CS + Law, CS + Commerce) is a strong differentiator.
4. Research access is **skewed to later years** — less early-stage opportunity than UNSW or Monash.
