# UNSW Sydney (University of New South Wales)

> **Location:** Sydney, NSW, Australia
> **Track:** Dual (Fall 2026 Term 2: May 14 / Fall 2027 Feb Intake: Oct-Dec 2026)
> **Program:** Bachelor of Computer Science (3yr, 144 UOC)

---

## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### Quantum Computing — Global Frontier
- Pioneering **Quantum Low-Density Parity-Check (QLDPC)** codes using Reed-Muller component codes for quantum error correction.
- Deep integration of quantum mechanics with theoretical CS — places UNSW at the **absolute frontier** of next-gen computational infrastructure.

### Trustworthy Systems & OS Security
- **LionsOS** — Novel seL4-based operating system for critical embedded systems. World-leading formal verification of OS kernels.
- Global leader in **Formal Methods, Operating Systems, and Trustworthy Systems** research.

### Digital Transformation
- Dedicated research themes in Digital Transformation and the Trustworthy Digital Society.
