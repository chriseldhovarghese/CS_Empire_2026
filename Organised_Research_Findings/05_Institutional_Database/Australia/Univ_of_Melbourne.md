# University of Melbourne

> **Location:** Melbourne, Victoria, Australia
> **Track:** Fall 2026 (Feb intake) / Fall 2027
> **Program:** BSc in Computing & Software Systems (Melbourne Model)

---

## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### AI & Machine Learning
- **AI Assurance Lab** — Led by **Benjamin Rubinstein** and **Sarah Erfani**. Focus: robustness, safety, and regulatory compliance of ML algorithms against adversarial attacks.
- Collaboration with **Defence Science and Technology Group** — ~40 academic staff validate AI technologies for safety/reliability.

### Quantum Computing
- **MIQT (Melbourne Initiative for Quantum Technology)** + **IBM Quantum Hub**.
- **Lloyd Hollenberg** and **Adrian Pearce** — Research: quantum algorithms for traffic optimization, bioinformatics, entanglement characterization.
- Undergrads can enroll in specific quantum software courses.

### Silicon / Chip Design
- **Nanophotonics and optoelectronics** — **James Bullock** and **Stan Skafidas**, researching 2D materials integration with silicon photonic integrated circuits for next-gen communications.

### Bio-Computation
- **Doherty Institute** — CRISPR-Cas13b models for antiviral therapeutics and targeted lipid nanoparticle delivery.

### Robotics
- **MSD Robotics Lab** — 6-axis robotic milling of complex geometries + assistive/rehabilitation robotics.
