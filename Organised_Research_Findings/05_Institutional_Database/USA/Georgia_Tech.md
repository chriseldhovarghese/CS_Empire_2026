# Georgia Institute of Technology (Georgia Tech)

> **Location:** Atlanta, Georgia, USA
> **Track:** Fall 2027 (EA: Oct 15 / RD: Jan 1)
> **Status:** Fall 2026 Closed
> **Program:** BS in Computer Science (College of Computing)

---

## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### AI & Machine Learning
- **School of Interactive Computing** — Specialized CS pathways + globally recognized online MSCS platform.
- AI research integrates computing systems with advanced ML — strong on **applied AI in engineering contexts**.

### Silicon / Chip Design — Apple Partnership
- **Silicon Jackets Initiative** — Student-run org bridging academic theory to industry tape-out.
- **ECE 4804: VLSI Design — Theory to Tapeout** — End-to-end VLSI pipeline from system spec to physical SoC fabrication/testing.
- **Apple New Silicon Initiative (NSI)** — Fellowships, expert guest lectures, access to state-of-the-art fabrication tech.
- **Key Insight:** Georgia Tech's **Apple partnership** gives undergrads direct industry mentorship no other school offers at the undergrad level.

### Cybersecurity
- **CIPHER Lab (Cybersecurity, Information Protection, and Hardware Evaluation Research)** — Hardware security, malware analysis, network forensics, reverse engineering.
- **CPSec Lab (Cyber-Physical Security)** — Security of embedded computers controlling physical processes (power grids, autonomous vehicles, infrastructure).

---

## 🎓 Undergraduate Research Architecture

| Program | Structure | Compensation | Access Level |
|:--------|:----------|:-------------|:-------------|
| **VIP (Vertically Integrated Projects)** | Multi-year teams of 10-20 students (sophomores→PhD) | Credit | Simulates industrial R&D; projects outlive individual students |
| **PURA (President's Undergrad Research Awards)** | Term-based | Salary subsidy + conference travel | Competitive |

- **VIP is the structural innovation.** Unlike 1-on-1 mentorship, VIP places undergrads in **hierarchical, multi-year teams** that simulate industry labs.
