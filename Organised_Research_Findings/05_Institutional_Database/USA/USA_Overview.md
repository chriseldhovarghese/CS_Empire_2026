## 🛂 Visa & Immigration Status (F-1)
- **Status Emoji:** 🟡 **Medium Risk (PR Chokepoint)**
- **Financial Proof:** USD 40,000 – 60,000 (I-20 baseline). No GIC requirement, but rigorous verification of liquid funds/sponsorship.
- **Work Rights:**
  - **In-Study:** 20 hrs/week on-campus.
  - **Post-Study:** 12-month OPT + 24-month STEM OPT extension (3 years total for CS/AI).
- **PR Pathway:** Primarily via **H-1B → EB-2/EB-3 Green Card**.
  - **Current Bottleneck:** H-1B lottery success rate ≈15% for entry-level. 10+ year backlog for Indian-born applicants in EB categories.

## 🎯 Exam Relevance for Top Tier (MIT, Stanford, UW, USU)

### SAT (🟢 Critical)
- **MIT:** Mandatory (reinstated). Mid-50% admitted = **1530–1580**, Math subscore near **780–800**. A 1550+ is squarely within range — removes test-score doubt but does NOT differentiate. Olympiads, original research, and "spike" activities differentiate.
- **Stanford:** Test-Optional, but historical submitted scores = **1510–1570**. A 1550+ is highly competitive but thousands of applicants match it. Essays and letters are the true differentiators.
- **UW Seattle (CS DTM):** Out-of-state Direct To Major CS acceptance = **~2%**. State enrollment caps dominate, meaning even a 1600 is not a safety net. GPA + course rigor paramount.
- **USU:** Functions as an **absolute safety school**. 1550+ triggers maximum merit-based aid and automatic honors admission.

| US University Tier | Competitive SAT for CS | 1550+ Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| HYPSM + CMU | 1510–1580 mid-50% | Baseline filter. Does NOT guarantee admission. |
| Top-20 Public (UW, UIUC, UT Austin) | 1370–1540 (CS skews higher) | Above typical, clearly advantageous |
| Top-100 (USU, Arizona State, etc.) | 1100–1350 | Auto-admit + merit scholarships |

### JEE (🟡 Contextual Signal Only)
- Top ~1000 AIR in JEE Advanced is treated like an **elite extracurricular** (akin to IMO/ISEF placement) at MIT/Stanford/Harvard.
- Does **NOT** replace SAT for any US university.
- Test-optional schools treat high JEE as a positive contextual factor, not a standalone credential.

### AP Exams (🟢 Highly Recommended)
- Scores of **4–5** in AP CS A, AP Calculus BC, AP Physics C demonstrate rigor and may grant college credit.
- Nearly universal acceptance for credit at US institutions.

## 🔴 Policy Traps & Hostility Signals
1. **The OPT Dead-End:** Relying solely on the H-1B lottery without a backup country (Canada/EU).
2. **"Duration of Status" Risk:** Proposed 2026 reforms may end 24/7 F-1 status, requiring fixed-term renewals.
3. **Consular Scrutiny:** Increased focus on "hostile attitudes" and social media screening at consulates.
