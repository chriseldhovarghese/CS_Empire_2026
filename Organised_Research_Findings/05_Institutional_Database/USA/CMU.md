## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### AI & Machine Learning
- Distributed across the **School of Computer Science**, **Robotics Institute**, and specialized AI ethics centers.
- Undergraduates access advanced pattern recognition, deep learning, and AI ethics research.
- **BAIR-equivalent depth** but with stronger robotics integration.

### Silicon / Chip Design — Open-Source Tape-Out Pioneer
- **Bill Nace** — Integrated open-source tools (Yosys, CocoTB, Verilator, OpenLane) into the curriculum.
- Students design and verify custom chips → submitted to **SkyWater Technology** for physical manufacturing.
- **ACTL (Advanced Chip Test Laboratory)** — Under Shawn Blanton. Undergrads detect, characterize, and mitigate IC failures using real fabricated silicon.
- **Key Insight:** CMU is the only target school where undergrads get **fabricated, functional silicon back** from their designs.

### Cybersecurity — Global Gold Standard
- **CyLab Security and Privacy Institute** — Director: Lorrie Cranor. Coverage: privacy policy usability, automated vulnerability identification, autonomous system security.
- **SPUR (Security and Privacy Undergraduate Research Scholars)** — Structured undergrad research program.
- **IT Lab: SSI (Summer Security Intensive)** — Fully funded 7-week fellowship at Heinz College. Students work with **USSTRATCOM, CISA, NCFTA** on operational cybersecurity.
- **Key Insight:** The SSI places undergrads directly into **US government cybersecurity operations** — no other program offers this.

## ⚠️ Strategic Notes

1. CMU SCS is arguably the **#1 overall CS school** (tied with MIT/Stanford) but with the strongest **systems/infrastructure** culture.
2. **CyLab + SSI** = unmatched for cybersecurity-focused applicants.
3. **Open-source tape-out** makes CMU uniquely accessible for hardware-interested students without industry connections.
4. The Robotics Institute makes CMU the top pick for **AI + Robotics convergence** alongside UW Seattle.
