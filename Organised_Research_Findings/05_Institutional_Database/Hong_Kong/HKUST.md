# HKUST (Hong Kong University of Science and Technology)

> **Location:** Hong Kong SAR
> **Category:** 🏆 HIDDEN GEM — Shenzhen Manufacturing Integration
> **Track:** Dual (Fall 2026 Late Round: June 30 / Fall 2027 Main: Jan 2027)
> **Program:** BEng in Artificial Intelligence / BEng with Extended Major in AI

---

## 🔬 Core Technical Specializations

### AI — Dedicated Undergraduate AI Degree
- HKUST offers a **dedicated BEng in Artificial Intelligence** — one of few globally at the undergrad level.
- Covers: mathematical foundations, deep learning, NLP, computer vision, robotics, data science, and **AI ethics**.
- Students in traditional engineering can attach an **Extended Major in AI** — infusing ML into civil, mechanical, etc.

### Research Infrastructure
| Lab/Center | Focus |
|:-----------|:------|
| **CAiRE (Center for AI Research)** | Speech processing, machine translation, beneficial AI governance |
| **CKSRI (Cheng Kar-Shun Robotics Institute)** | Multi-disciplinary autonomous systems |
| **HKUST-DJI Joint Innovation Lab** | Unmanned aerial vehicles |
| **HKUST-Huawei Joint Lab** | Telecommunications AI |
| **HKUST-DiDi Joint Lab** | Autonomous driving |
| **HKUST-NAVER/LINE AI Lab** | Search/platform AI |

- **Key Insight:** The corporate co-branded labs (DJI, Huawei, DiDi) give undergrads **direct industry research access** — unique at this level.

1. **4% acceptance rate** approaches Ivy territory — do NOT treat HKUST as a safety.
2. **JEE scores ARE used** as competitive differentiators — leverage this if available.
3. **DJI/Huawei/DiDi labs** = unmatched for applied robotics and autonomous driving at the undergrad level.
4. **Shenzhen proximity** = hardware prototyping and manufacturing pipeline within a 1-hour train ride.
5. The **dedicated AI BEng** is a rare offering — most top schools only have CS with an AI specialization track.
